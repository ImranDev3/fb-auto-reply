Frequently Asked Questions

What is AutoReply Pro?
AutoReply Pro is a SaaS tool that automatically replies to your Facebook Messenger and WhatsApp messages based on keywords you set. It works 24/7 without you being online.
Do I need coding knowledge?
No! AutoReply Pro is completely no-code. Just sign up, connect your page, add keyword rules, and you're done. It takes less than 5 minutes.
Is there a free plan?
Yes! Our Free plan includes 5 auto-reply rules and Messenger support. No credit card required. Upgrade anytime for more features.
Does it work when I'm offline?
Absolutely! That's the whole point. Once you set up your rules and enable Away Mode, the bot replies automatically 24/7 — even while you sleep.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. No contracts, no hidden fees. Your data remains accessible on the Free plan.
How do I connect my Facebook Page?
After signing up, go to Profile > Page Details. Enter your Page Access Token from Facebook Developer Console. We have a step-by-step guide to help you.
