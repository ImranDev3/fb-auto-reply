Refund Policy

Last updated: January 2024

1. Free Trial

AutoReply Pro offers a free plan that never expires. You can use the Free plan indefinitely without any payment. No credit card is required to sign up.

2. Paid Subscriptions

When you upgrade to a paid plan (Pro or Enterprise), you will be billed according to the plan you selected (monthly or annually).

3. 7-Day Money-Back Guarantee

We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee on all paid plans. If you are not satisfied with our service within the first 7 days of your paid subscription, you can request a full refund.

  • Refund requests must be made within 7 days of the initial payment
  • Contact us at support@autoreplypro.com with your account email
  • Refunds are processed within 5-10 business days

4. After 7 Days

After the 7-day period:

  • Monthly subscriptions: No refund for the current billing period, but you can cancel to prevent future charges
  • Annual subscriptions: Prorated refund may be available at our discretion

5. How to Cancel

  • You can cancel your subscription at any time from your Dashboard → Profile
  • Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period
  • You will retain access to paid features until the end of your billing period
  • After cancellation, your account reverts to the Free plan

6. Non-Refundable Situations

  • Account terminated due to Terms of Service violation
  • Service issues caused by third-party platforms (Facebook, WhatsApp outages)
  • Failure to set up the service correctly (we offer free setup support)

7. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, email us at support@autoreplypro.com with:

  • Your registered email address
  • Reason for the refund request
  • Date of purchase

We will respond within 24 hours and process eligible refunds within 5-10 business days.

8. Contact

Questions about our refund policy? Contact us at: support@autoreplypro.com
